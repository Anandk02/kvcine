Refund Policy

At Kvcine.com, we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience in learning dance. We understand that sometimes things don't go as planned, and we want to make sure that you feel confident in your purchase.

Cancellation Policy

  1. If you need to cancel a class or workshop, we require at least 24 hours' notice before the start of the class or workshop. If you cancel within this time frame, you will be eligible for a full refund.
  2. If you cancel less than 24 hours before the start of the class or workshop, you will not be eligible for a refund.

Refund Policy

  1. Registration Fees: Registration fees are non-refundable. This fee covers the cost of processing your registration and securing your spot in the class.
  2. Cancellation by Client: If you need to cancel your registration, you must notify us at least 7 days prior to the start of the class. In this case, we will provide a full refund of your payment. If you cancel within 7 days of the class start date, we will refund 50% of your payment.
  3. Cancellation by Instructor: In the unlikely event that we have to cancel the class, we will provide a full refund of your payment.
  4. Missed Classes: We understand that sometimes unforeseen circumstances may prevent you from attending a class. Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds for missed classes.
  5. To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at kvcineinfo@gmail.com. We will review your request and may ask for additional information to help us better understand the reason for your dissatisfaction.
  6. If your refund request is approved, we will issue a refund to your original payment method within 7-10 business days

Exceptions

There may be some cases where we are unable to provide a full refund, such as in the following situations:
  1. You have attended more than one class or workshop and are requesting a refund for all of them.
  2. You have used a discount code or promotional offer that makes the class or workshop non-refundable.
  3. You have violated our terms of service or code of conduct, and we have terminated your access to the class or workshop
In these cases, we may offer a partial refund or credit towards future classes or workshops.

Changes to Refund Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. If we make any changes, we will post the updated policy on our website and notify our customers via email

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please don't hesitate to contact us at kvcineinfo@gmail.com. We are always here to help!
